## Total Revenue
Sum of net revenue from FactSales.

## Total Cost
Sum of total cost from FactSales.

## Total Profit
Sum of profit from FactSales.

## Margin %
Total Profit divided by Total Revenue.

## Order Count
Distinct count of OrderID.
## Revenue LY
Revenue for the same period in the previous year.

## Revenue YoY
Absolute year-over-year revenue change.

## Revenue YoY %
Percentage change in revenue compared to the same period last year.

## Revenue MTD
Month-to-date revenue.

## Revenue YTD
Year-to-date revenue.

## Revenue PM
Revenue for the previous month.

## Revenue MoM
Absolute month-over-month revenue change.

## Revenue MoM %
Percentage change in revenue compared to the previous month.
## Note

Time intelligence calculations are primarily implemented via the Time Intelligence calculation group.

Some standalone measures (e.g. Revenue YTD, Revenue YoY) may exist for validation or transitional purposes but are not the preferred approach for ongoing model design.
