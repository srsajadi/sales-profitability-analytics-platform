# KPI Definitions

## Revenue Metrics
- Gross Revenue: Quantity × Unit Price
- Net Revenue: Gross Revenue − Discount
- Total Revenue: Sum of Net Revenue

## Cost & Profit
- Total Cost: Quantity × Unit Cost
- Profit: Net Revenue − Total Cost
- Margin %: Profit / Net Revenue

## Discount Metrics
- Discount Amount: Total discounts applied
- Discount %: Discount Amount / Gross Revenue

## Volume Metrics
- Total Quantity: Sum of units sold
- Order Count: Number of unique orders

## Efficiency Metrics
- Average Order Value: Revenue / Orders
- Revenue per Customer

## Time Intelligence
- Revenue LY (Last Year)
- Revenue YoY
- Revenue YoY %
- Revenue MoM
- Revenue YTD
- Rolling 3 Month Revenue

## Analytical Metrics
- Profit per Product
- Margin by Category
- Low Margin Product Count
